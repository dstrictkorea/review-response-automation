# Policy 10. Reporting and Evidence

## 1. Reporting Principle

Do not overclaim from small samples.

## 2. Evidence Labels

| Label | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Strong | repeated across many reviews/channels |
| Moderate | several examples within same branch |
| Weak | one or two reviews only |
| Observation | needs human validation |

## 3. Required Report Fields

- date range
- branch
- channel coverage
- total reviews
- average rating
- response rate
- negative review rate
- high-risk count
- top categories
- representative reviews
- action items

## 4. AI Recommendation Rule

AI recommendation must include:
- evidence count
- affected branch/channel
- confidence
- suggested owner
- expected operational impact
