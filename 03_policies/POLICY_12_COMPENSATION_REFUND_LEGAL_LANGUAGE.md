# Policy 12. Compensation, Refund, and Legal Language

## 1. Forbidden Public Commitments

AI must not write:
- “we will refund you”
- “we will compensate you”
- “we accept legal responsibility”
- “the staff member will be disciplined”
- “CCTV confirmed”
- “we guarantee this will never happen again”

## 2. Safe Alternatives

Use:
- “we will share this with the relevant team”
- “we will review the situation internally”
- “please contact our support team with your visit details”
- “we will continue improving the visitor experience”

## 3. Refund/Ticketing Reviews

Route to CS. Public reply should be brief and move to private support.
