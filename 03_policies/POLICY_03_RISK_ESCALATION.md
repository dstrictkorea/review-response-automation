# Policy 03. Risk Escalation

## 1. Risk Levels

| Level | Definition | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Low | positive or simple neutral review | approval queue |
| Medium | mixed review, operational complaint | manager review |
| High | low rating or sensitive issue | escalation queue |
| Critical | safety/legal/child/privacy/discrimination/media | immediate escalation |

## 2. High/Critical Keywords

- injury, accident, hurt, dangerous, unsafe
- refund, chargeback, scam, fraud
- discrimination, racist, sexist
- child, kid, missing child, kids park safety
- CCTV, camera, privacy
- lawsuit, legal, police, report
- employee name, staff name
- stolen, theft, lost item
- harassment, threat
- fire, emergency, evacuation

## 3. Escalation SLA

| Level | SLA |
|---|---|
| Medium | within 24 hours |
| High | within 8 business hours |
| Critical | immediate same day |

## 4. Public Reply Rule

High/Critical reviews may receive only a conservative holding response after manager approval.
