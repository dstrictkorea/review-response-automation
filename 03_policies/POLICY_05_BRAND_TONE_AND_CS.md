# Policy 05. Brand Tone and CS

## 1. Brand Voice

ARTE MUSEUM replies should be:
- calm
- premium
- warm
- concise
- accountable
- non-defensive
- culturally natural

## 2. Forbidden Tone

- sarcastic
- argumentative
- copy-paste generic
- overly emotional
- robotic
- legalistic
- blaming customer

## 3. Standard Structure

Positive:
1. Thank you
2. Mention experience
3. Invite again

Mixed:
1. Thank you
2. Acknowledge good point
3. Recognize inconvenience
4. Improvement statement
5. Invite again

Negative:
1. Apology
2. Acknowledge specific issue
3. Internal review/improvement
4. Support channel if needed
5. Avoid overpromising

## 4. Museum-Specific Words

Use carefully:
- immersive experience
- media art
- exhibition
- visit
- on-site team
- visitor flow
- guidance
- guest experience
