# OPS 01. Daily Operations SOP

## Morning

1. Check workflow health.
2. Check failed review collection.
3. Check high/critical review queue.
4. Check pending approvals older than SLA.
5. Check Google publish failures.
6. Check manual publish queue.
7. Check spend alerts.

## During Day

- Approve low-risk replies.
- Review medium/high-risk replies.
- Manually publish TripAdvisor/Naver/OTA replies.
- Mark manual published status.
- Escalate sensitive cases.

## End of Day

- Confirm no critical review remains unassigned.
- Confirm all Google publish jobs succeeded or are queued.
- Record operational notes.
