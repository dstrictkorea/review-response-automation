# OPS 02. Approval SOP

## 1. Low Risk

- Read original review.
- Check AI draft.
- Make minor edit if needed.
- Approve.
- Google: publish through system.
- Manual channels: copy/paste.

## 2. Medium Risk

- Confirm complaint category.
- Ensure reply acknowledges issue.
- Remove overpromising.
- Approve or escalate.

## 3. High/Critical

- Do not publish immediately.
- Check internal support channel.
- Assign CS/manager.
- Use holding reply only after approval.

## 4. Approval Checklist

- correct language
- correct branch
- no hallucinated facts
- no refund/legal promise
- no staff identity
- no CCTV/privacy statement
- tone not defensive
