# OPS 08. Training Guide

## What Staff Must Understand

1. AI writes drafts, not final truth.
2. High-risk reviews must be escalated.
3. Do not promise refunds or compensation.
4. Do not mention CCTV.
5. Do not argue publicly.
6. Manual channels still require platform-side posting.
7. Every manual publish must be marked complete.
8. If unsure, escalate.

## Training Exercise

- Approve 10 positive reviews.
- Edit 10 mixed reviews.
- Escalate 10 high-risk reviews.
- Detect 10 forbidden phrases.
- Publish 5 manual channel replies in test mode.
