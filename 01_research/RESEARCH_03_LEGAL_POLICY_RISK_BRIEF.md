# Legal and Platform Policy Risk Brief

## 1. Practical Risk View

This system manages public customer-facing replies. Risk is not only technical; it includes brand, legal, privacy, consumer protection, and platform enforcement risk.

## 2. High-Risk Areas

| Risk | Example | Control |
|---|---|---|
| Fake engagement | creating reviews or incentivizing ratings | Permanent exclusion from scope |
| Platform automation violation | bypassing login/CAPTCHA | No bypass; manual mode fallback |
| Public admission of liability | “we are legally responsible” | Legal phrase guardrail |
| Refund promise | “we will refund you” | Escalate to CS |
| Privacy leak | CCTV, visitor path, staff identity | Public reply ban |
| Discrimination | nationality/gender/race assumptions | Language based on text only |
| Employee issue | staff name in review | Human review |
| Child safety | Kids park complaints | Critical escalation |
| Accident/injury | slip, fall, injury | Critical escalation |
| Defamation response | arguing with reviewer | Calm non-defensive tone |

## 3. Policy Line

Automating “drafting and routing” is acceptable.  
Automating “unreviewed public commitments” is not acceptable.

## 4. Escalation Rule

When in doubt, do not publish. Route to human.
